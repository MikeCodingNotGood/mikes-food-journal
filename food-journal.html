Food Journal

Connect your Dropbox to keep your data backed up privately — or skip and use locally.

1
Go to dropbox.com/developers/apps and click Create app.
2
Choose Scoped access → App folder. Name it anything (e.g. FoodJournal). Click Create app.
3
On the app's Settings page, scroll to OAuth 2 → under Generated access token click Generate. Copy that token.
4
Paste it below and hit Connect.
